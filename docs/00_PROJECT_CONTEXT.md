# 00 — Project Context

## One-line definition

Xreviews is a complaint-only review platform and negative-experience data company.

## What we are building

A web-first MVP where users can search and create review subjects, submit only negative/problem-focused reviews, attach evidence, and view public complaint/risk pages. Businesses can claim official accounts, reply to reviews, and publish improvement posts.

## Why this exists

The current review market is polluted by praise, advertising, paid reviews, influencer content, and platform incentives. Consumers increasingly want to avoid the worst option, not find a perfect option.

Xreviews inverts the review market:

- Other platforms ask: “Was it good?”
- Xreviews asks: “What should others avoid?”

Core user insight:

> 혹시 리뷰를 찾아볼 때 1점부터, 혹은 나쁜 후기부터 먼저 찾아보는 편이신가요?

Many users already check bad reviews before good reviews. Xreviews productizes that existing habit. “Looking from 1-star reviews first” is a copy device describing behavior on other platforms; Xreviews itself does not use star ratings.

## Radical product law

Positive reviews are not allowed.

This is not a nice-to-have. This is the category-defining rule.

## Operating principle

Surface radical. Internal system disciplined.

The brand must be sharp enough to be noticed. The backend must be structured enough to survive abuse, takedowns, business disputes, fraud, and legal pressure.

## MVP categories

1. Medical clinics (`medical_clinic`)
   - Dermatology
   - Korean medicine clinic
   - Cosmetic/aesthetic/surgery clinic
2. Real estate agencies
3. Auto repair shops

## Out of scope for MVP

- Person reviews
- General restaurants/cafes
- Shopping product reviews
- Full AI legal judgment
- Full payment integration
- Public scraping from Naver/Kakao/Google
- Native mobile app

## Core user types

| User type | Description | Key jobs |
|---|---|---|
| Anonymous visitor | Searches and reads public complaint pages | Avoid bad decisions |
| Consumer user | Writes complaint reviews and uploads evidence | Warn others, document experience |
| Business owner | Claims official account | Reply, explain, improve |
| Admin/moderator | Reviews submissions and disputes | Keep platform usable and defensible |

## Product moat

The moat is not low-star scraping or star ratings.

The moat is:

1. Evidence-backed complaint data
2. Risk taxonomy
3. Business replies and dispute history
4. Improvement posts
5. Trust scoring and negative signal graph

## North Star Metric

Verified risk-avoidance actions.

A risk-avoidance action is a user behavior indicating that Xreviews helped avoid or reconsider a decision:

- Subject page view from search
- Save/bookmark
- Share
- Return visit to same subject
- Click “write complaint”
- Click “compare similar complaints”
- Alert subscription
