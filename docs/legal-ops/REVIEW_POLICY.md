# Xreviews Review Policy

> Draft for closed beta operations. This document is a product/legal-ops working draft and does not replace advice from a licensed attorney.

## 1. Core rule

Xreviews does not accept positive reviews.

Good reviews, recommendation reviews, and praise-centered reviews belong elsewhere. Xreviews is for negative consumer experiences: problems, complaints, mismatches, risks, and reasons another person should be cautious.

## 2. Required review basis

A complaint must be based on the author's actual experience.

The author must:

- describe what happened
- choose at least one risk tag
- confirm legal responsibility for false content
- avoid exaggeration beyond what they personally experienced
- avoid exposing unnecessary personal information

## 3. Accepted content

Accepted complaint themes include:

- pricing mismatch
- refund or contract issue
- waiting time
- pressure sales
- hygiene/facility issue
- ad mismatch
- suspected fake listing
- estimate mismatch
- repair without consent
- insufficient explanation
- process failure
- repeated inconvenience

## 4. Positive-review prohibition

Blocked examples:

- "좋아요"
- "친절해요"
- "추천합니다"
- "만족합니다"
- "최고예요"
- "또 갈게요"
- "믿고 가세요"
- "여기 잘합니다"

Allowed mixed examples:

- "상담은 친절했지만 결제 금액이 처음 안내와 달랐습니다."
- "시설은 괜찮았지만 환불 안내가 불명확했습니다."
- "응대는 나쁘지 않았지만 예약 시간보다 1시간 넘게 대기했습니다."

The difference is simple: if the core message is praise, it is blocked. If the core message is a concrete problem, it can be reviewed.

## 5. Forbidden expressions

Do not use definitive or insulting labels such as:

- 사기
- 범죄
- 돌팔이
- 쓰레기
- 무조건 사기
- 악질

Use experience-based language instead:

- "처음 안내와 달랐습니다."
- "환불 기준 설명이 불명확했습니다."
- "허위매물로 의심되는 정황이 있었습니다."
- "견적과 실제 청구가 달랐습니다."

## 6. False statements

Fabricated, exaggerated, or knowingly misleading content is prohibited.

Xreviews may request evidence, hide a complaint, mark it disputed, or move it to removed status if falsehood risk is high.

## 7. Insults and personal attacks

Users may criticize a business process or consumer experience.

Users may not:

- insult a person
- attack appearance, identity, nationality, gender, disability, or religion
- encourage harassment
- expose personal contact information
- target individual staff unnecessarily

## 8. Privacy

Do not include:

- private phone numbers
- private emails
- full names of unrelated individuals
- resident registration numbers or equivalent identifiers
- home addresses
- account numbers
- patient identifiers
- screenshots containing unrelated personal data

## 9. Medical clinic guide

Medical clinic complaints must focus on consumer process:

- consultation process
- price explanation
- refund/contract issue
- pressure sales
- waiting time
- hygiene/facility
- ad mismatch

Do not make definitive claims about:

- treatment effect
- diagnosis correctness
- medical malpractice
- side-effect causation
- "this doctor caused X"

Safer phrasing:

- "상담에서 가격 설명이 충분하지 않았습니다."
- "광고에서 본 내용과 실제 안내가 달랐습니다."
- "예약 시간보다 오래 대기했습니다."

## 10. Real estate guide

Focus on:

- suspected fake listing
- price change
- management fee explanation mismatch
- room condition mismatch
- contract pressure
- photo/reality mismatch

Avoid naming private individuals unless necessary and policy-safe.

## 11. Auto repair guide

Focus on:

- suspected over-repair
- estimate mismatch
- recurring issue after repair
- insufficient parts explanation
- repair without prior consent
- weak invoice/specification

Use documents such as estimates or invoices when available.

## 12. Publication

New complaints are saved as `pending`.

Only administrator-approved `published` complaints appear on public pages.

Other statuses are not public:

- `pending`
- `disputed`
- `hidden`
- `removed`
