# Xreviews Terms of Service Draft

> Draft for closed beta operations. This document is a product/legal-ops working draft and does not replace advice from a licensed attorney.

## 1. Service identity

Xreviews is a complaint-only review platform for negative consumer experiences.

Xreviews does not accept positive reviews, recommendation reviews, or praise-centered reviews. The service exists to collect and organize problem-focused consumer risk signals.

## 2. Beta status

During closed beta, Xreviews may limit:

- invited users
- available categories
- available regions
- review publication
- business claim processing
- evidence review
- ranking visibility

Closed beta access may be revoked if a user abuses the service, submits prohibited content, or creates operational/legal risk.

## 3. User responsibility

Users must submit content based on their own actual experience.

Before submitting a complaint, the user must confirm:

> 나는 이 불만이 내 실제 경험에 기반하며, 허위 사실 작성 시 법적 책임이 나에게 있음을 확인합니다.

The author is responsible for false, fabricated, misleading, defamatory, or privacy-violating submissions.

## 4. Complaint-only rule

Users may not submit:

- praise-only reviews
- recommendation reviews
- advertising-like reviews
- content written primarily to promote a business
- star ratings or average-rating style evaluations

Mixed content may be accepted only when the core submission clearly describes a problem, inconvenience, mismatch, risk, or reason another consumer should be cautious.

## 5. MVP categories

Closed beta supports only:

- `medical_clinic`: hospital/clinic complaints focused on consultation, price, refund, pressure sales, waiting, hygiene/facility, and ad mismatch
- `real_estate`: real estate complaints focused on suspected fake listings, price changes, contract pressure, management fee explanation mismatch, and photo/reality mismatch
- `auto_repair`: auto repair complaints focused on suspected over-repair, estimate mismatch, recurring issues, insufficient parts explanation, and repair without prior consent

Person review targets are not allowed.

## 6. Medical category limits

For medical clinics, Xreviews focuses on consumer process experiences.

Users must not make definitive claims about:

- treatment effect
- diagnosis correctness
- medical malpractice
- medical causation
- side-effect causation
- personal attacks against a specific doctor or staff member

Moderators may require rewriting, hide, dispute, or remove content that creates medical/legal risk.

## 7. Evidence files

Users may submit evidence such as receipts, invoices, estimates, contracts, photos, messages, or PDFs.

Evidence files are private-by-default.

Xreviews stores evidence metadata and private R2 object keys. Xreviews does not publish evidence files by default and does not expose public evidence URLs on public pages.

Evidence may be reviewed by authorized operators for moderation, disputes, legal requests, and safety operations.

## 8. Review lifecycle

New complaints are not public by default.

Review states:

- `draft`: not submitted or internal draft state
- `pending`: submitted and awaiting review
- `published`: approved for public display
- `disputed`: under dispute or right-of-reply/legal review
- `hidden`: temporarily or operationally hidden
- `removed`: no longer publicly available due to policy, legal, or safety reasons

Xreviews treats removal as a status change, not a business-controlled deletion right.

## 9. Moderation authority

Xreviews administrators may:

- approve a complaint for publication
- hide a complaint
- mark a complaint disputed
- move a complaint to removed status
- request more evidence
- review private evidence for moderation purposes
- reject prohibited content
- maintain audit logs for sensitive actions

Xreviews may act without prior notice when content creates immediate safety, privacy, legal, or operational risk.

## 10. Business rights and limits

Businesses may apply for an official account.

Approved official business accounts may:

- display an official account badge
- write official responses
- publish improvement posts
- submit correction/evidence materials through allowed channels

Businesses may not:

- delete consumer reviews
- pay to delete reviews
- hide reviews
- change review status
- identify or expose reviewers
- threaten users through official replies

Business subscription or official account status is a response/improvement/analysis right, not a deletion right.

## 11. Prohibited content

Users may not submit:

- fabricated claims
- definitive criminal accusations without process
- insulting or degrading labels
- personal information
- threats or harassment
- hate or discriminatory content
- sexual, violent, or exploitative content
- spam, duplicate submissions, or competitor abuse

See `PROHIBITED_CONTENT_POLICY.md` for detailed operating standards.

## 12. Audit and safety logs

Xreviews may log sensitive actions, including review publication, hiding, disputes, removal, evidence access, business claim decisions, and legal request handling.

Logs are used for operational safety, abuse prevention, legal defense, and accountability.

## 13. Changes during beta

Because this is a closed beta, Xreviews may update policies, UI copy, review standards, and moderation workflows as risk patterns emerge.

Material policy updates should be reflected in the legal-ops documentation before broader launch.
