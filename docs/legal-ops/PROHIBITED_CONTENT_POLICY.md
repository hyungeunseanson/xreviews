# Xreviews Prohibited Content Policy

> Draft for closed beta operations. This document is a product/legal-ops working draft and does not replace advice from a licensed attorney.

## 1. Purpose

Xreviews is intentionally sharp, but it is not a place for fabrication, harassment, doxxing, or reckless legal claims.

This policy defines content that must be blocked, rewritten, hidden, disputed, or moved to removed status.

## 2. Defamation-risk claims

Users must avoid presenting serious accusations as established fact when they have not been verified through an appropriate process.

High-risk patterns:

- "This business is a criminal operation."
- "They committed fraud."
- "The doctor is a quack."
- "They intentionally scammed me."
- "They are criminals."

Safer pattern:

- describe the experienced facts
- name the mismatch
- provide evidence when available
- use "의심", "정황", "안내와 달랐음" rather than definitive criminal labels

## 3. Criminality as fact

Do not state crime as fact unless there is a reliable official source and legal review.

For beta moderation, definitive criminal accusations should usually be:

- blocked before publication
- rewritten into experience-based language
- marked disputed
- hidden pending review
- escalated for legal review

## 4. Insulting labels

Do not allow direct degrading labels such as:

- 사기
- 범죄
- 돌팔이
- 쓰레기
- 악질
- 양아치

Moderators should ask the author to describe the concrete issue instead.

## 5. Personal information

Prohibited:

- national ID numbers
- patient registration numbers
- private phone numbers
- private emails
- full home addresses
- account/payment numbers
- unrelated private message content
- photos showing unrelated people or personal documents

If personal data appears, hide immediately and redact or remove before any possible publication.

## 6. Hate and discrimination

Do not allow content attacking protected or sensitive attributes, including:

- race
- nationality
- ethnicity
- gender
- sexual orientation
- disability
- religion
- age
- disease or medical condition

## 7. Sexual, violent, or exploitative content

Do not allow:

- explicit sexual content unrelated to the consumer issue
- graphic violence
- threats
- encouragement of self-harm or harm to others
- exploitation of minors

Escalate immediately if there is imminent harm.

## 8. Threats and extortion

Prohibited:

- "Refund me or I will destroy you online."
- "Pay me to remove this."
- "I will keep posting until you pay."
- threats to visit, harass, or reveal personal information

These may require account restriction and incident response.

## 9. Competitor abuse

Competitors may not use Xreviews to attack other businesses.

Suspicious signals:

- repeated posts against nearby competitors
- no direct consumer experience
- identical wording across subjects
- business-linked email domains
- coordinated timing

Moderators should request evidence or reject.

## 10. Spam and repeated posts

Prohibited:

- duplicate complaints
- automated submissions
- keyword stuffing
- repeated low-effort posts
- promotional content
- irrelevant links

## 11. Medical-specific prohibited content

Medical clinic complaints must not definitively claim:

- wrong diagnosis
- malpractice
- treatment caused injury
- guaranteed treatment failure
- personal doctor attack

Rewrite around consumer process:

- consultation
- price/refund
- waiting
- hygiene/facility
- ad mismatch
- pressure sales

## 12. Enforcement actions

Moderators may:

- block submission
- request rewrite
- request evidence
- keep pending
- publish
- hide
- mark disputed
- move to removed
- restrict account access
- escalate to incident response

All sensitive enforcement actions should be logged.
