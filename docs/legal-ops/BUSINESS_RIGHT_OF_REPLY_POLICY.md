# Business Right of Reply Policy

> Draft for closed beta operations. This document is a product/legal-ops working draft and does not replace advice from a licensed attorney.

## 1. Principle

Xreviews gives businesses a right to respond, not a right to delete.

Businesses can explain, correct, dispute, and show improvements. They cannot remove consumer complaints by payment, pressure, or subscription status.

## 2. Official account claim

A business may request an official account for a subject page.

The claim should include:

- business name
- applicant name
- contact email
- optional phone
- explanation of relationship to the subject
- verification note or supporting context

During closed beta, document upload for claim verification may be manual or deferred.

## 3. Admin claim review

Only administrators may:

- approve a claim
- reject a claim
- revoke a claim
- create or activate a business profile
- change a user role to business as an admin action

All decisions should be logged in `audit_logs`.

## 4. Official badge

An approved business profile may display:

> 공식 인증 사업자

This badge means Xreviews verified the official account relationship. It does not mean:

- the business is recommended
- the business is safe
- complaints are false
- Xreviews guarantees service quality

## 5. Official response

Approved businesses may respond to published complaints for their own approved subject.

Official responses may:

- explain context
- apologize
- correct factual details
- dispute a claim
- describe resolution steps

Official responses may not:

- threaten the author
- reveal reviewer identity
- include private personal data
- demand deletion
- advertise unrelated services
- offer payment for removal

## 6. Improvement posts

Approved businesses may publish improvement posts explaining what changed after complaints.

Allowed themes:

- hygiene/facility improvements
- price policy changes
- refund policy clarification
- staff training
- process changes
- other operational improvements

Improvement posts must not be written like promotional ads.

## 7. Evidence and correction materials

Businesses may submit correction context or evidence through approved flows.

Closed beta rule:

- business evidence submission may be handled manually
- businesses do not get public evidence URL access
- businesses do not get reviewer identity access
- business access to consumer evidence requires explicit future permission checks

## 8. Dispute flow

Recommended flow:

1. Business submits official response or dispute note.
2. Admin reviews claim, complaint, evidence, and audit history.
3. Admin may mark the complaint `disputed`, `hidden`, `published`, or `removed`.
4. Author may be asked for evidence or clarification.
5. Public visibility remains controlled by admin moderation policy.

## 9. Subscription boundary

Paid plans may provide:

- official badge
- official replies
- improvement posts
- analytics
- alerts
- multi-location management

Paid plans must never provide:

- review deletion
- review hiding
- ranking manipulation
- reviewer identity
- private evidence access without permission checks
