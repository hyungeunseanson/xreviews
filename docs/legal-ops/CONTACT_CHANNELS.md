> Phase 12 operating registry. Placeholder addresses must be replaced with real monitored inboxes before closed beta launch.

| Purpose | Placeholder | Owner before launch |
|---|---|---|
| General support | `support@xreviews.example` | Founder/operator |

| Business official account inquiries | `business@xreviews.example` | Business ops owner |
| Security or evidence exposure reports | `security@xreviews.example` | Incident owner |

## 2. Launch requirement

Before staging invite emails are sent:

- [ ] Replace placeholder emails with real monitored inboxes
- [ ] Confirm who receives each inbox
- [ ] Confirm response SLA for legal/privacy/security reports
- [ ] Confirm escalation path for severe incidents
- [ ] Update terms, privacy policy, takedown policy, beta plan, and incident response docs with real addresses

## 3. Minimum SLA targets for closed beta

| Channel | Initial response target |
|---|---:|
| Security/evidence exposure | same day |
| Legal notice | same day |
| Privacy request | 2 business days |
| Business dispute | 2 business days |
| Beta feedback | 3 business days |
| General support | 3 business days |

## 4. Handling rules

- Do not ask users to send unnecessary sensitive documents by email.
- Do not forward evidence URLs in public or broad channels.
- Keep legal notices and privacy requests in restricted-access channels.
- Record incident summaries in the incident response log.
- For urgent evidence exposure, pause affected flows before debating growth impact.
